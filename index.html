------------Aquabots Fish Tracking---------------

Problem / Soulution

For years, fishermen have relied solely on sites that provide outdated information for things such as fish variety and fish locations. Fishermen have struggled since, with finding proper fishing sights and at times would come home with barely any catch. Our goal is to use SoFi, a fish robot with the ability to record the sea, map areas, and sense if fish went missing. Using the information SoFi can give us we would upload the data to this website, for all fishermen to access.









About our website

This website is to help fishfolk know how many fish there are in one area. By using a robot fish named SoFi, we will track fish so you can know where the right place for you is to fish!
