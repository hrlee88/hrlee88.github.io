Fish Robots

The SoFi robot (soft robotic fish) is extremely useful to help researchers, ocean explorers and fishermen. This fish robot has many technical capabilities including a live camera, tracker, and mapping ability. It also looks like a fish to make integration into fish populations easier. This robot will help save time, resources and money.
